Lucid Dream
"A dream where you realize you are dreaming and can control the experience." “清醒梦是你在梦中意识到自己在做梦，并能够控制梦境内容。”
Fantasy Dreams
"A dream filled with magical, surreal, or impossible events." “幻想梦充满魔幻、超现实或不可能的场景。”
Nightmares
"A dream filled with fear or distress, often reflecting deep anxieties." “噩梦通常充满恐惧或焦虑，反映潜意识中的压力。”
Falling Dreams
"A dream where you feel like you are falling endlessly before suddenly waking up." “坠落梦是指你在梦中感觉自己不断下坠，通常会在突然惊醒时结束。”
